Our Sweet Story

From a small home kitchen in Sargodha to a brand loved for its soft cakes, creative cupcakes, dreamy donuts, and elegant bouquets — every treat is made with love.

✨ How It All Began

Baking Mellow was born in Sargodha in 2024 — a small home bakery with a big dream. What started as a passion for baking quickly grew into a brand that brings joy to every celebration.

Our mission is simple: make every celebration sweeter with fresh, affordable, beautifully crafted treats. Every product is made with love, care, and the commitment to deliver premium quality.

We believe that the best ingredients create the best memories — and we pour our hearts into every cake, cupcake, donut, brownie, bouquet, and gift basket we make.

Our Journey

2024 — The Beginning

Baking Mellow started as a small home bakery in Sargodha with just a few cake recipes and a big dream.

2024 — First Orders

Our first birthday cakes and cupcakes became an instant hit among friends and family. Word started spreading.

2025 — Growing Brand

We launched our full menu — cakes, cupcakes, donuts, brownies, bouquets, and gift baskets. Our online store took off.

2025 — Loved by Thousands

Today, Baking Mellow is a trusted name in Sargodha, delivering happiness to thousands of customers every month.

2026 & Beyond — The Future

We're expanding our menu, launching new collections, and bringing the sweetness of Baking Mellow to more cities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas? +
We currently deliver across Sargodha and surrounding areas. For specific locations, please contact us via WhatsApp or phone.
How far in advance should I place a custom cake order? +
We recommend placing custom orders at least 3–5 days in advance. For large or complex designs, please allow up to 7 days.
Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options? +
Yes! We offer gluten-free and vegan versions of most products. Please let us know your dietary requirements when ordering.
Can I customize the design of my cake? +
Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs. You can send us your ideas, photos, or themes, and we'll create a cake that perfectly matches your vision.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, JazzCash, and EasyPaisa. For online orders, we also support card payments via our secure checkout.
Do you offer gift wrapping and personalized messages? +
Yes! All gift baskets and bouquets come with beautiful wrapping. We also offer custom message cards for a personal touch — just mention it in your order.
