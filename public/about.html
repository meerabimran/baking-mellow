✨ Our Story

Baking Mellow was born in Sargodha in 2024 — a small home bakery that grew into a brand loved for its soft cakes, creative cupcakes, dreamy donuts, and elegant bouquets.

Our mission is simple: make every celebration sweeter with fresh, affordable, beautifully crafted treats.

Every product is made with love, care, and the commitment to deliver premium quality. We believe that the best ingredients create the best memories.
